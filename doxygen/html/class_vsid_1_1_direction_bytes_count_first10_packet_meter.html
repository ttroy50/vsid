Detailed Description

Average Bytes per direction of the first 10 packets.

Should be good for protocols that have setup flows

Currently doesn't take into account flow setup with TCP. Hence the 10 packets i.e. 5 per direction. May overcompensate for UDP flows where the setup handshake may only be a 3 or 4 way handshake
