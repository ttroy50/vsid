\subsection{Detailed Description}
Average Bytes per direction of the first 10 packets.

Should be good for protocols that have setup flows

Currently doesn't take into account flow setup with T\-C\-P. Hence the 10 packets i.\-e. 5 per direction. May overcompensate for U\-D\-P flows where the setup handshake may only be a 3 or 4 way handshake
